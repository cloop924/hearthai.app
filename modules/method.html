Method

Three pillars that lead to Thai fluency

HearThai is built around the three skills that make Thai usable in real conversation: vocabulary you can recall instantly, grammar you can see, and pronunciation that respects tones.

Vocab
Pillar 1
Fast vocabulary recall
Hear Thai words, choose the meaning, and move on. The app adapts to what you get right and wrong so common words become automatic instead of “I know this, let me think…”.
Grammar
Pillar 2
700+ grammar formulas
See Thai grammar as clear formulas: subject, verb, object, particles, and aspect markers. Tap through 700+ patterns with examples so you start recognizing structures instead of guessing.
Pronunciation
Pillar 3
Pronunciation through listening
Every word and sentence is backed by premiere Thai audio. You hear tones in real speech and match what you say to what you hear, not to rough romanization.
