Questions

FAQ

What is Scenario Mode?
Scenario Mode places you inside 100 real Thai locations — food stalls, markets, hotels, temples, hospitals, taxis, and more. Each scenario gives you the vocabulary for that place, real conversations, a word-by-word breakdown of every sentence, and a quiz. It's designed to prepare you for a specific real-world interaction before you're in it.
How many locations are in Scenario Mode?
100 real Thai locations, organized across categories including Food, Transport, Shopping, Healthcare, Accommodation, and more.
Is HearThai good for total beginners?
Yes. HearThai works from day one — core vocabulary, real audio, and grammar structure from the start. You don't need to know the Thai alphabet to begin. Writing mode helps when you're ready for it.
Does it replace a class or tutor?
No — and it doesn't try to. HearThai fits alongside any course or tutor as a serious training tool. It reinforces vocabulary recall, grammar structure, pronunciation, and real-world readiness.
How much grammar does it cover?
750+ sentence formulas covering Thai structure from beginner through advanced. Every pattern has examples, audio, and a visual breakdown of how the sentence is built.
Is the audio real or AI?
HearThai uses premium AI-generated Thai audio — not a synthetic text-to-speech voice. The result is consistent, high-quality pronunciation across all 52,000 audio files.
iPhone only?
Currently iPhone only. iPad optimization is included. Android is planned.
What does it cost?
HearThai offers a free tier to start. Full access is available as a monthly subscription or a one-time lifetime purchase — both available inside the app after download.
