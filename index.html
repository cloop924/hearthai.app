Daily Use

A simple routine that fits real life

HearThai is designed so you can make real progress in short sessions. You can use it entirely on its own or alongside any other Thai course, textbook, or tutor.

Short vocab bursts

Open the vocab trainer whenever you have a spare moment. Run quick rounds of 10–20 words, mark the ones you know, and favorite the ones that need work.

Focused grammar time

A few times a week, open the grammar formulas and work through several cards in order. Listen to the examples, read the Thai, and notice how the pattern repeats.

Listening & speaking practice

Replay audio for sentences and words that matter to you. Copy what you hear out loud, then test yourself in conversation with Thai speakers around you or online.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about how HearThai fits into your Thai learning and what to expect when you start.

Do I need to know any Thai before I start?
No. HearThai works for complete beginners and for learners who already know a lot of words. Beginners can focus on basic vocabulary, simple patterns, and pronunciation. Experienced learners can use it to organize what they know and train recall speed.
Does HearThai replace textbooks or classes?
HearThai is designed to sit beside whatever you already use. Textbooks and classes explain ideas; HearThai gives you the repetition and audio you need so those ideas show up in your speech.
How much grammar does the app cover?
HearThai includes more than 700 grammar formulas covering everyday sentence patterns, core particles, common aspect markers, and structures that appear frequently in real Thai. New patterns can be added over time without changing how the app feels.
Is this only for iPhone and iPad?
HearThai is built primarily for iPhone and iPad users. The experience is designed around mobile use so you can practice listening, vocabulary, and grammar wherever you are.
How much time do I need to use it each day?
Even a few minutes a day is enough to run several vocab rounds or review a set of grammar formulas. You can take longer sessions when you have them, but HearThai is designed to fit into short gaps in your day.
